Valassis Digital Brand Style Guide

This style guide outlines the Valassis Digital brand identity. Copy writers, designers, product and corporate marketing, and outside creative vendors should follow these guidelines to create consistency across all Valassis Digital assets and build brand recognition among our customers.

Overview

This is the definitive resource for Valassis Digital's styles and best practices. We encourage you to reference the Valassis Digital Style Guide when producing collateral for internal or external use. If you need design assistance or guidance contact dl_branding@Valassis Digital.com.
